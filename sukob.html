Sukob

Synopsis:A Filipino couple working overseas return to their hometown to get married. But a deadly curse follows the couple, based on the superstition that marriage should not take place within a year of the death of an immediate relative.

Director:Chito S. Roño
Writers:Chito S. Roño & Chriz Martinez
Genre:Drama/Horror/Mystery/Thriller
